How It Works

Our streamlined process makes it easy to apply for and receive funding tailored to the needs of Asian American creators.

Application Process

1. Submit Application

Fill out our straightforward online application with information about yourself and your creative work.

2. Credit Check

We perform a soft credit check (doesn't affect your score) to evaluate eligibility.

3. Application Review

Our team reviews your credit history, portfolio, and business plan.

4. Get Approved

Receive your loan offer with clear terms and repayment schedule.

5. Receive Funding

Funds transferred within 24-48 hours after accepting terms.

6. Join Our Community

Access resources and events to help your creative career flourish.
